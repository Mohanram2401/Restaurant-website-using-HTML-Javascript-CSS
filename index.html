About Us

Resort hotels cater to the needs of holiday makers and tourists. They are located in the areas or natural beauty like lakes or beaches. Recreational facilities like swimming pools, tennis courts, golf courses, surfing, etc. are also provided in resort hotels. Summer, winter, all seasons, hill and health are some of the important types of resorts. Airport hotels are usually situated near domestic or international airports. Such hotels generally cater to tourist travelling by airways. Another type of hotel is residential hotel. Charges in such hotels are based on monthly, half yearly and yearly. These hotels are situated in important cities. The customers can live in such hotel as long as they wish.
